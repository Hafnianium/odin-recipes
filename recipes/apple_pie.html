Apple Pie

This is a picture of apple pie.

This is a practice site of the apple pie recipe. Below there is an unordered list of the ingredents:

  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 5 granny smith apples
  • Other stuff

Next are steps to make the pie:

  1. Preheat oven
  2. Melt butter in skillet
  3. Remove skillet from heat
  4. Put stuff in skillet
  5. Bake skillet in oven
